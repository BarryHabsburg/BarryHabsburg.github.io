Biographic Page:

Name is Barry Daemi, and I reside in Plano, Texas. I attended Southern Methodist University as a undergraduate from Aug. 2016 to May 2020, and graduated with a B.S. in Computation and Applied Mathematics (with departmental distinction in Mathematics) and B.A. in Economics (with departmental distinction in Economics) May, 2020.
‍
I was inducted into three honor societies at Southern Methodist University, Alpha Chi National College Honor Scholarship Society, Golden Key International Honour Society and Omicron Delta Epsilon – Honour Society Economics (nominated twice and ducted twice).

I conducted research as a undergraduate in Von Neumann Morgenstern Portfolio Theory, and as a graduate student devoted most of my time to independent inquiries into various topics, such as supervised and non-supervised machine learning, and Monte carlo methods and Simulation.

During the summer of 2022, I was a Data Analyst intern in the Stregatic Risk Management (SRM) department at Ally financial; I developed a logistic regression model to estimate the effectiveness of collection tools, and of which I used to devised a plan to improve Ally Financial's collection efforts by replacing less effective collections treatments with more effective collection treatments; e.g. if a group of customers response better to text message but are currently being treat with dialer, then replace the dialer treatment with a text message collections treatment.
